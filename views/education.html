Overview of the University of Information Technology

University of Information Technology (IT University), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM) is a public university training in information and communication technology (IT & ICT) established under the Decision No. Decision No. 134/2006/QD-TTg dated June 8, 2006 of the Prime Minister. As a member school of VNU-HCM, the University of Information Technology is responsible for training high-quality information technology human resources, actively contributing to the development of Vietnam's information technology industry, and at the same time progressing to the next level. conduct scientific research and transfer advanced information technology, especially towards applications to contribute to promoting the industrialization and modernization of the country.
After more than 10 years of construction and development, the field of IT University owns facilities including study, research and working areas which are invested in spacious and modern construction with a total area of ​​over 14 hectares on campus. urban area VNU-HCM.
